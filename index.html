FIRE! BE AWARE OF IT


WHY?
Fire though only deadly depending on your situation, is a very deadly and life-changing incident to be caught in. In 2021 alone, 1,353,500 fires resulted in 3,800 civilian deaths and 14,700 injuries.
Even if the initial fire doesn't cause death it possibly damages both yourself and the envoirnment. Not even proffesionals are safe as, in 2021, 19,200 injuries or 32 percent of all reported firefighter injuriesâ€”occurred at the the fire site.
If not personal danger fire is also a great envoirnmental detriment when unchecked. Fire can destroy homes, wildlife habitats our Earth's own atmosphere and timber all while polluting the air with emissions harmful to human health.

HOME FIRES
From 2015-2019 More than one-quarter (26%) of reported fires occured in homes. Even worse, three-quarters (75%) of civilian fire deaths and almost three-quarters (72%) of all reported injuries were caused by home fires.
